No matter how well things are going for me I always circle back to depression
I have no reason to be depressed. I have a good job, nice house, and a beautiful girlfriend yet I find myself sitting on the edge of my bed almost every night staring at the floor in a major depressive episode. 

I have been diagnosed with depression for years but I don’t want to take anti depressants because I work in healthcare and know first hand how badly they can affect you. 

Does it ever get better? I feel like anyone else would be satisfied with their life, but I’m just not…
